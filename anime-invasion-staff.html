Join the Team

Want to Become a Staff Member?

We're always looking for dedicated, passionate members to help shape the Anime Invasion experience. If you love the game and want to make a difference in the community, we want to hear from you.

  • Active in the community — regular player and Discord presence
  • Positive track record — no active bans or punishments
  • Mature and fair — ability to stay calm under pressure
  • Available — minimum 5 hours per week commitment
  • Team player — willing to collaborate with existing staff
Apply via Discord
Application Process
How it works
01
Join the Discord
Head to our official Discord server and find the #staff-applications channel.
02
Submit Your Application
Fill in the application form — your Roblox username, experience, and why you want to join.
03
Review Period
Senior staff review all applications within 7 days. You'll be notified of the outcome.
04
Trial Period
Accepted applicants begin as Trial Moderator. Prove yourself and earn your permanent rank.
